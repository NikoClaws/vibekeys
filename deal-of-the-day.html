Full Product Review

Sound Quality: 9/10

The Beats Studio Pro deliver exceptional audio quality that finally sheds the bass-heavy reputation of older Beats products. These headphones feature a completely redesigned acoustic architecture with custom 40mm drivers that produce a balanced, detailed sound signature.

The low end is punchy and controlled without overwhelming mids or highs. Vocals come through crystal clear, and there's impressive separation across the frequency range. Whether you're listening to hip-hop, classical, rock, or podcasts, these headphones adapt beautifully.

Spatial Audio Support: When paired with Apple devices, you get dynamic head tracking spatial audio that creates an immersive 3D sound experience. It's particularly impressive for movies and supported music content.

Active Noise Cancellation: 8.5/10

The ANC on the Studio Pro is excellent, though not quite class-leading. It effectively blocks out airplane noise, office chatter, and city sounds. There are three modes to choose from:

  • Noise Cancelling: Maximum isolation for focused listening
  • Transparency: Hear your surroundings clearly
  • Off: Pure audio without processing

The Transparency mode is particularly well-implemented, making conversations natural without removing the headphones.

Comfort & Design: 9/10

Beats has significantly improved the comfort compared to previous Studio models. The redesigned headband distributes weight evenly, and the plush ear cushions use breathable materials that don't get hot during extended listening sessions.

At 260g, they're lighter than the AirPods Max (385g) but slightly heavier than the Sony XM5 (250g). Most users can wear them comfortably for 3-4+ hours without fatigue.

The build quality is premium throughout, with soft-touch plastics and sturdy hinges. They fold flat for easy storage in the included carrying case.

Battery Life: 9/10

With up to 40 hours of battery life with ANC off (or 24 hours with ANC on), the Beats Studio Pro outlast most competitors. A quick 10-minute charge gives you 4 hours of playback – perfect for those moments when you forgot to charge overnight.

Charging is via USB-C, and the headphones support both wired USB-C audio (for lossless playback) and 3.5mm analog connection.
